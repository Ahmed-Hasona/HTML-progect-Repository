A database management system is important because it effectively manages the data and allows users to perform multiple tasks with ease.

In a single software program, a database management system stores, organizes and manages a vast volume of information.

Use of this system increases operational efficiency and reduces overall costs.

Database management systems are essential to businesses and organizations because they offer a highly efficient method for managing multiple data types.

For example, the human resources department of a organization uses the database to maintain employee documents, distribute employee legal information and produce updated recruiting reports.

Relational databases use several tables, and in addition to data elements, describe relationships between them using a schema.
